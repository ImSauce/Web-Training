JUJUTSU KAISEN

Jujutsu Kaisen dives into a dark and gripping world where malevolent curses run rampant, feeding off human suffering and negativity. The story follows Yuji Itadori, a high school student with extraordinary physical abilities, who stumbles into the dangerous realm of jujutsu sorcery after a fateful encounter with a cursed object—the finger of the powerful Curse, Ryomen Sukuna. To save his friends from a monstrous threat, Yuji consumes the cursed finger, becoming the host for Sukuna and thrusting himself into a deadly supernatural conflict. Under the guidance of the eccentric yet powerful jujutsu sorcerer Satoru Gojo, Yuji enrolls in Tokyo Jujutsu High, where he trains to harness his new powers and combat the growing tide of curses. As he forges unbreakable bonds with fellow sorcerers Megumi Fushiguro and Nobara Kugisaki, Yuji must confront his own humanity and the perilous balance between life and death, all while navigating a world where curses can be more terrifying—and human—than they appear.
