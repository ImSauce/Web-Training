GIRLS UND PANZER

Girls und Panzer takes place in an alternate world where the art of tank warfare, known as "sensha-dō," is a celebrated and traditional martial discipline practiced by high school girls across the globe. The story follows Miho Nishizumi, a reluctant but talented tank commander who transfers to Ōarai Girls’ Academy, a school that has long abandoned the sensha-dō sport. However, when the academy’s survival is threatened, Miho is thrust back into the world she tried to leave behind, leading a ragtag team of friends and classmates into intense tank battles against skilled rival schools. With the stakes higher than ever, Miho must confront her past, embrace her leadership, and prove that the unlikeliest of teams can triumph through unity, strategy, and sheer determination. The film blends thrilling armored combat sequences with themes of friendship, personal growth, and the unbreakable spirit of teamwork.
