ARKNIGHTS

In a world devastated by natural disasters and plagued by the mysterious infection known as Oripathy, Arknights unfolds as a gripping tale of survival, sacrifice, and moral ambiguity. The story centers around Rhodes Island, a pharmaceutical and paramilitary organization dedicated to finding a cure for Oripathy and offering hope to the infected, who face societal discrimination and harsh conditions. Players take on the role of the enigmatic "Doctor," a tactical genius suffering from amnesia, who leads a diverse team of Operators, each with their own unique combat abilities and backstories, through intense strategic battles and missions. As Rhodes Island battles against hostile factions like the violent Reunion Movement and uncovers the deep-rooted corruption of world powers, the line between heroism and villainy blurs.
